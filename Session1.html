One Piece

Author: Eiichiro Oda

About

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It has been serialized in Shueisha's shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen Jump since July 1997, with its individual chapters compiled into 106 tankōbon volumes as of July 2023.

One Piece Arc in Order

  1. Romance Dawn Arc (Episodes 1-3)
  2. Orange Town Arc (Episodes 4-8)
  3. Syrup Village Arc (Episodes 9-19)
  4. Baratie Arc (Episodes 19-30)
  5. Arlong Arc (Episodes 31-44)

One Piece Characters

  • Monkey D. Luffy
  • Roronoa Zoro
  • Nami
  • Robin
  • Hancock


Genres

Shonen manga, Action manga, Comedy, Fantasy
